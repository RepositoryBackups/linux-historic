Finding Help

The GNOME Help Browser (see Figure 2-7) is one of the more useful utilities at your command.

In fact, since this application displays not only GNOME-related help but also info pages and man pages, the GNOME Help Browser can be an invaluable resource for learning about both GNOME and your Red Hat Linux system. (Info pages and man pages are concise help documents which are usually installed on a Linux system. These help documents are covered more extensively in Chapter 12.)

Figure 2-7. The GNOME Help Browser

You can open the GNOME Help Browser by double-clicking on its launcher on the desktop or from the Panel by dragging your cursor to the item Help System on the Main Menu Button.
